Web Development

As Microsoft Student Ambassadors at Gautam Buddha University, we actively teach web development and organize workshops regularly to provide hands-on learning experiences. Our sessions cover a wide range of technologies essential for building modern web applications, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React for front-end development.

App Development

As Microsoft Student Ambassadors at Gautam Buddha University, we also teach app development and conduct workshops regularly to offer practical insights into mobile and web-based app creation. Our sessions cover essential technologies like Java, Kotlin, and Swift for native app development, along with frameworks like Flutter and React Native.

Data Structure & Algorithm

As Microsoft Student Ambassadors at Gautam Buddha University, we also teach Data Structures and Algorithms, conducting workshops regularly to help students master these fundamental concepts. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

As Microsoft Student Ambassadors at Gautam Buddha University, we also teach Machine Learning, regularly conducting workshops to introduce students to this exciting field. Our sessions cover foundational concepts using popular frameworks like TensorFlow, Keras, and Scikit-learn.

As Microsoft Student Ambassadors at Gautam Buddha University, we also teach Cloud Computing, organizing workshops regularly to equip students with cloud platform skills. Our sessions cover core concepts like IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS, while introducing various cloud architectures.

Blockchain

As Microsoft Student Ambassadors at Gautam Buddha University, we also teach Blockchain technology, regularly conducting workshops to help students understand decentralized systems. Our sessions cover key concepts such as distributed ledgers, cryptography, and smart contracts.
